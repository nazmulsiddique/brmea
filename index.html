About :

It’s our pleasure to inform you that Bangladesh Refrigerator Manufacturers Association was incorporated under the companies Act (Act XVIII) of 1994. The certificate of Incorporation No. TO-732/10 and the date of incorporation is 03 March, 2010. BRMA is run by a 7 –member Board of Directors lead by the President.

BRMA was formed in 2010 as a separate body to represent solely the Refrigerator sector of Bangladesh. After the formation, it under took activities to look after the interest of the Refrigerator sector of the country. BRMEA is putting diligent efforts to enhance productivity of its members, enhance social compliance status and workers welfare, diversify export market and better market access of the country’s Refrigerators to Asian, Middle East and African Market. BRMA has been tirelessly striving to promote the Refrigerator sector of Bangladesh both at home and abrod.
